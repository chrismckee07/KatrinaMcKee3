Sydney never expected that anything would be hidden beneath the Medina Grand Hotel…

After relocating from New York to Adelaide, Sydney is just starting to settle into her new role as the manager of the Medina Grand Hotel.

Life in Adelaide is quieter, less chaotic than her previous life in New York, and Sydney enjoys it that way.

That is until Sydney inadvertently discovers that the Medina Grand holds mysteries to be uncovered.

Transported into a world of secrets and portals, Sydney meets an arresting team of explorers in the throes of a mission to break a code and find keys, seemingly lost to time.

Answers lie deep within the pages of century old journals and long family histories, spread across Melbourne and Adelaide. Sydney and the team must be the first to break the code, as the world could well be at stake.
