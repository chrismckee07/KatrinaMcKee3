PORTAL

ISBN: 9781923172012

Sydney never expected that anything would be hidden beneath the Medina Grand Hotel…

After relocating from New York to Adelaide, Sydney is just starting to settle into her new role as the manager of the Medina Grand Hotel.

Life in Adelaide is quieter, less chaotic than her previous life in New York, and Sydney enjoys it that way.

That is until Sydney inadvertently discovers that the Medina Grand holds mysteries to be uncovered.

Transported into a world of secrets, portals and strange occurrences, Sydney meets an arresting team of explorers in the throes of a mission to find a code, seemingly lost to time.

Answers lie deep within ancient catacombs and long family histories and Sydney must be the first to find them, or she may be trapped forever in the mazes she explores.

“A mystical mystery of delight for lovers of great sci-fi… Thoroughly enjoyed it!” - Alex, Readalot reviewer
